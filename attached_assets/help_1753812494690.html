How to Use the Laundry Booking System

Getting Started
  1. Create an Account: Click "Register" to create your student account with a username and password.
  2. Login: Use your credentials to access the booking system.
  3. Book a Slot: Select your preferred time, wash type, and payment method.
  4. Complete Payment: Confirm your booking by completing the payment process.
  5. Use the Laundry: Arrive on time for your booked slot.
